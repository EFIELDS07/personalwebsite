DISCOVER UMD

Why UMD?

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because of its beautiful natural surroundings, inspiring lake views, and a strong Business Finance program. The campus is welcoming and vibrant, and I knew it would give me the academic challenges and growth I wanted.

Why I Love Duluth

Duluth is more than just a campus—it’s a community. I love the local food, cozy cafes, scenic trails, and the chance to explore culture, nature, and the outdoors all in one place. The city perfectly balances adventure and comfort, making it an ideal place for students to thrive.
